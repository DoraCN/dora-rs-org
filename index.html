Welcome to Dora-rs.org, the organizational home of the Dora-rs project. As a non-profit organization, we are dedicated to advancing the development and adoption of Dora-rs, an open-source project focused on data orchestration and real-time analytics. Our organization oversees the project's governance, community engagement, and strategic direction.

Our mission encompasses promoting sustainable development practices, fostering collaboration among contributors, and ensuring the long-term growth of the Dora-rs ecosystem. We facilitate coordination between developers, maintain project standards, and support the community's needs.
